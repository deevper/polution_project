What is Pollution?

Pollution refers to the introduction of harmful substances or contaminants into the environment, causing adverse effects on the quality of air, water, soil, or ecosystems, and posing risks to human health and the well-being of other living organisms.

Air Pollution

Air pollution is the presence of harmful substances in the air, compromising its quality and posing health and environmental risks.
